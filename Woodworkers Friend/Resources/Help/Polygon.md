# Polygon Calculator

## Overview
Calculate angles and dimensions for multi-sided projects like hexagonal tables, octagonal mirrors, or any polygonal design.

## What It Calculates

- **Interior angles**: Angle at each vertex
- **Miter angles**: For cutting joints
- **Side length**: Based on diameter or radius
- **Perimeter**: Total outside measurement

## Required Inputs

- **Number of Sides**: 3 to 20 sides
- **Radius or Diameter**: Distance from center
- **Material Thickness**: For joint calculations (optional)

## Common Polygons

### Triangle (3 sides)
- Interior angle: 60°
- Miter angle: 30°

### Square (4 sides)
- Interior angle: 90°
- Miter angle: 45°

### Pentagon (5 sides)
- Interior angle: 108°
- Miter angle: 54°

### Hexagon (6 sides)
- Interior angle: 120°
- Miter angle: 60°

### Octagon (8 sides)
- Interior angle: 135°
- Miter angle: 67.5°

## Formulas

**Interior Angle:**
180° × (n - 2) / n

**Miter Angle:**
90° - (Interior Angle / 2)

**Side Length:**
2 × Radius × sin(180° / n)

## Tips for Cutting

### Miter Saw Setup
- Set saw to miter angle
- Cut each piece with same angle
- Test fit before gluing

### Precision
- Small errors multiply across sides
- Test fit frequently
- Make a template
- Label pieces

### Common Projects
- Hexagonal tables
- Octagonal frames
- Multi-sided boxes
- Segmented turning
