# Drawer Calculator

## Overview
The Drawer Calculator provides precise dimensions for building drawers using various construction methods.

## Calculation Methods

### 1. Face Frame Standard
Traditional method for face frame cabinets with full overlay doors.

### 2. Face Frame Inset
For inset drawer fronts in face frame construction.

### 3. Frameless Standard
European-style frameless cabinet construction.

### 4. Frameless 5-Piece
Five-piece drawer front construction for frameless cabinets.

### 5. Half-Blind Dovetails
Classic dovetail joinery with exposed tails on drawer sides.

### 6. Through Dovetails
Traditional through dovetails visible on drawer front.

### 7. Box Joint
Finger-jointed construction with interlocking pins.

### 8. Rabbet Joint
Simple rabbet joint construction.

### 9. Dado Joint
Dado joints for drawer box construction.

### 10. Lock Miter
Locking miter joints for drawer boxes.

## Required Inputs
- **Cabinet Opening Width**: Inside dimension of cabinet
- **Cabinet Opening Height**: Vertical opening size
- **Cabinet Depth**: Front to back dimension
- **Material Thickness**: Drawer box material thickness
- **Construction Method**: Select from 10 methods above

## Results Provided
- Drawer front dimensions
- Drawer box dimensions (front, back, sides)
- Bottom panel dimensions
- Cut list for all components

## Tips
- Measure cabinet openings carefully
- Account for drawer slide clearance
- Consider grain direction on drawer fronts
- Use consistent material thickness for easier construction
