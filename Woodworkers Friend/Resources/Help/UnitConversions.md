# Unit Conversions

## Overview
Quickly convert between imperial and metric measurements for your woodworking projects.

## Supported Conversions

### Length
- **Inches ↔ Millimeters**
- **Inches ↔ Centimeters**
- **Feet ↔ Meters**
- **Yards ↔ Meters**

### Common Conversions
- 1 inch = 25.4 mm
- 1 foot = 30.48 cm
- 1 inch = 2.54 cm

## How to Use

1. **Enter Value**: Type the number to convert
2. **Select From Unit**: Choose current unit
3. **Select To Unit**: Choose desired unit
4. **Result**: Conversion shown instantly

## Precision

- Results shown to 4 decimal places
- Round as needed for your project
- More precision available if needed

## Quick Reference Table

### Inches to Millimeters
- 1/16" = 1.6 mm
- 1/8" = 3.2 mm
- 1/4" = 6.4 mm
- 1/2" = 12.7 mm
- 1" = 25.4 mm

### Common Workshop Sizes
- 3/4" plywood = 19mm
- 1/2" plywood = 12.5mm
- 2x4 lumber = 38mm × 89mm
- 4x8 sheet = 1220mm × 2440mm

## Tips

### When to Convert
- Buying imported tools
- Following metric plans
- Mixing measurement systems
- International projects

### Best Practices
- Stick to one system per project
- Convert at planning stage
- Round to workable precision
- Double-check critical dimensions
